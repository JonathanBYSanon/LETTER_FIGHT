Letter Clash - Game Rules

👤 - Two players take turns selecting letters to create a hidden word.

🔒 - The hidden word must contain all the chosen letters. If a player cannot form a valid word, they must either think of another or challenge their opponent.

⚔ - A player may challenge their opponent at any time, forcing them to reveal their word.

✅ - If the revealed word is valid, the challenged player earns points equal to the word's length.

🚫 - If the word is invalid (missing chosen letters or not a real word), the challenger earns points equal to the number of letters chosen so far.

🏆 - The first player to reach the target score wins the game.
